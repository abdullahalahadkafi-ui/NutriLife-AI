Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2026 | Transparency in Health Data Science

1. Introduction

Welcome to NutriLife AI. This Privacy Policy outlines our commitment to protecting your privacy and managing your data with the highest level of security and transparency. At NutriLife AI, we operate as a health-tech evaluation platform, providing Body Mass Index (BMI) calculations and dietary guidance through advanced automated algorithms.

By accessing or using our services at nutrilife-ai.onrender.com, you signify your acceptance of this policy. If you do not agree with any terms in this policy, please refrain from using our health tools and services.

2. Information Collection & Usage

NutriLife AI collects minimal data required to provide instant health reports. The information is divided into two categories: Personally Identifiable Information (PII) and Technical Data.

Health Metrics: We collect your weight, height, age, and gender. This data is used solely to execute the BMI mathematical formula and cross-reference WHO health standards. We do not store this data permanently on our databases. Once you close your session, your inputs are cleared from our active memory.

"NutriLife AI is committed to a 'Zero-Retention' policy for health metrics to ensure your personal biological data remains private."

3. Log Files and Analytical Data

NutriLife AI follows a standard procedure of using log files. These files log visitors when they visit websites. The information collected by log files includes internet protocol (IP) addresses, browser type, Internet Service Provider (ISP), date and time stamp, referring/exit pages, and possibly the number of clicks. This technical information is used for analyzing trends, administering the site, and improving user experience.

4. Google AdSense & Cookies

NutriLife AI is an authorized publisher for Google AdSense. Google, as a third-party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads on our site. Google's use of the DART cookie enables it to serve ads to our users based on their visit to NutriLife AI and other sites on the Internet.

Users may choose to decline the use of the DART cookie by visiting the Google ad and content network Privacy Policy at the following URL – https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads. Please note that we have no access to or control over cookies used by third-party advertisers.

5. GDPR Data Protection Rights

We would like to make sure you are fully aware of all of your data protection rights. Every user is entitled to the following:

  • The right to access: You have the right to request copies of any personal data we hold.
  • The right to rectification: You have the right to request that we correct any information you believe is inaccurate.
  • The right to erasure: Under certain conditions, you can request that we erase your personal data.

6. Children's Privacy Protection

Protecting children's privacy is our priority. NutriLife AI does not knowingly collect any Personal Identifiable Information from children under the age of 13. If you think that your child provided this kind of information on our website, we strongly encourage you to contact us immediately and we will do our best to promptly remove such information from our records.
