Who We Are

Welcome to NutriLife AI, a pioneering health-technology platform dedicated to revolutionizing personalized nutrition and wellness. Based in Bangladesh, we combine advanced Data Science with deep nutritional insights to provide real-time health evaluations. Our platform is designed to empower individuals with the data they need to live a balanced, disease-free life.

In an era of misinformation and generalized health advice, NutriLife AI stands out by offering scientifically-backed calculations and customized dietary guidance. We believe that technology should be a companion in your fitness journey, making complex data easy to understand and act upon.

Our AI-Driven Mission

Our mission is simple: To democratize health awareness. We understand that traditional nutritionist consultations can be expensive and inaccessible. By utilizing Artificial Intelligence (AI), we have built an automated system that analyzes your BMI (Body Mass Index) and physical metrics to generate immediate, actionable health reports.

Whether you are looking to lose weight, build muscle, or manage a chronic condition like diabetes, our AI algorithms are trained to guide you toward the right nutritional path. We strive to reduce global obesity and malnutrition rates through digital innovation and constant research.

Scientific Accuracy & Standards

Every calculation performed on NutriLife AI follows the strict standards set by the World Health Organization (WHO). Our BMI categories and dietary suggestions are cross-referenced with medical journals to ensure that you receive the most accurate information possible.

We leverage modern web technologies to ensure that our tools are not only accurate but also incredibly fast and mobile-responsive. At NutriLife AI, your digital experience is as important to us as your health results.
